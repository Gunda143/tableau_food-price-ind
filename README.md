**Food Prices Analysis in India - Tableau Dashboard**

**1.Overview**
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing food prices across India. The objective is to transform raw food price data into meaningful insights through data visualization, helping users understand regional price variations, category-wise trends, and city-level comparisons.

The project demonstrates practical skills in data cleaning, exploratory analysis, dashboard development, and visual storytelling using Tableau.

**2.Project Objectives**

#Analyze food prices across different regions of India.
#Compare average prices across food categories.
#Identify pricing trends in milk and dairy products.
#Study food price variations among major Indian cities.
#Build interactive dashboards that support data-driven insights.

Dashboard Highlights

**Sheet 1.Average Food Category Prices:** Provides a comparison of average prices across various food categories, helping identify the most and least expensive categories.


**Sheet 2.Milk & Dairy Product Analysis**:Focuses on milk and dairy products to understand pricing patterns and regional differences.


**Sheet 3.City-wise Food Price Comparison**:Compares food prices across major Indian cities, highlighting geographical variations and market trends.

Key Insights
Food prices vary significantly across different regions of India.
Certain categories consistently show higher average prices than others.
Dairy products exhibit noticeable price differences across cities.
Urban markets often display distinct pricing patterns compared to other regions.
Interactive visualizations make it easier to identify trends and compare locations.
